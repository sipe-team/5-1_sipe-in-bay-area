# Week 5

## Plan

For Week 5, we held an English Night instead of a regular article discussion session.

The goal was to create a more casual environment where participants could speak English naturally, not only about software engineering topics but also about everyday life. We wanted everyone to practice speaking without feeling too much pressure, while still keeping the English-only rule as much as possible.

Since there were drinks and snacks left after the event, we may be able to hold another English Night near the end of May. A smaller home party format is also possible for around five people.
